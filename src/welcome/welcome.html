Ask Gemini sits one keystroke away — type a question,
highlight text, or fire from anywhere in your browser.

Quick start
Global shortcut Press Ctrl+Shift+L from any tab to summon the popup instantly.
Smart selection Highlight any text on any page, right-click, and choose Ask Gemini — it auto-fills.
Prompt templates Type / in the popup to pick a saved snippet. Manage them in Settings → Templates.
Model selector Switch between Fast, Think, and Pro right in the popup — no detours to Gemini.
